# WoW Classic Professions Guide

Complete reference for all WoW Classic professions including trainer locations and ideal farming zones.

---

## Gathering Professions

### Mining

**Skill Level Progression & Farming Zones:**

- **1-65 (Copper Ore)**: All starter zones - Durotar, Mulgore, Tirisfal Glades, Elwynn Forest, Darkshore, Dun Morogh
  - **Special Location**: Venture Co. Mine in Durotar (good for experienced players)

- **65-125 (Tin & Silver Ore)**: Hillsbrad Foothills, Redridge Mountains, Ashenvale, The Barrens

- **125-175 (Iron & Gold Ore)**: Arathi Highlands, Desolace, Thousand Needles

- **175-245 (Mithril & Truesilver Ore)**: The Hinterlands, Tanaris

- **245-300 (Thorium & Rich Thorium Ore)**: Un'Goro Crater, Blasted Lands, Felwood, Eastern Plaguelands, Winterspring, Burning Steppes

**Special Trainer Locations:**
- No special locations required - all ranks trainable in major cities

---

### Herbalism

**Skill Level Progression & Farming Zones:**

- **1-70**: Starter zones - Durotar, Mulgore, Tirisfal Glades, Elwynn Forest, Teldrassil, Dun Morogh
  - **Herbs**: Peacebloom, Silverleaf, Earthroot

- **70-115**: The Barrens, Silverpine Forest, Loch Modan, Darkshore
  - **Herbs**: Mageroyal, Briarthorn, Stranglekelp (requires 85+)

- **115-170**: Hillsbrad Foothills, Wetlands, Stonetalon Mountains
  - **Herbs**: Bruiseweed, Wild Steelbloom, Stranglekelp, Kingsblood (125+), Liferoot (150+)

- **170-205**: Stranglethorn Vale, Arathi Highlands
  - **Herbs**: Kingsblood, Liferoot, Fadeleaf, Goldthorn, Khadgar's Whisker (185+)

- **205-230**: Tanaris, Searing Gorge
  - **Herbs**: Purple Lotus, Firebloom

- **230-270**: The Hinterlands
  - **Herbs**: Sungrass, Purple Lotus, Ghost Mushroom (245+), Golden Sansam

- **270-300**: Felwood
  - **Herbs**: Sungrass, Gromsblood, Golden Sansam, Dreamfoil, Mountain Silversage, Plaguebloom

**Special Trainer Locations:**
- No special locations required - all ranks trainable in major cities

---

### Skinning

**Skill Level Progression & Farming Zones:**

- **1-75**: Durotar, Dun Morogh (starting areas)

- **75-155/165**: The Barrens, Loch Modan, Wetlands

- **155/165-205**: Thousand Needles, Arathi Highlands

- **205-300**: Feralas, Un'Goro Crater

**Important Notes:**
- You must have a skinning knife in your bag to skin mobs
- Skill requirement formulas:
  - Below 100: ((skill)/10)+10
  - Above 100: (skill)/5

**Special Trainer Locations:**

**Expert (150+):**
- **Horde**: Dranh (Camp Taurajo, The Barrens)
- **Alliance**: Available in major cities

**Artisan (225+):**
- **Horde**: Kulleg Stonehorn (Camp Mojache, Feralas)
- **Alliance**: Available in major cities

## Crafting Professions

### Alchemy

**Recommended Combination:** Herbalism (saves gold on materials)

**Special Trainer Locations:**

**Expert (150+):**
- **Horde**: Undercity
- **Alliance**: Darnassus

**Artisan (225+):**
- **Horde**: Swamp of Sorrows
- **Alliance**: Feralas

**Farming Zones:** See Herbalism section for herb farming locations

---

### Blacksmithing

**Recommended Combination:** Mining

**Ore Farming References:**
- Copper Ore farming (1-65)
- Tin Ore farming (65-125)
- Iron Ore farming (125-175)
- Mithril Ore farming (175-245)
- Thorium Ore farming (245-300)

See Mining section for specific zone locations.

**Special Trainer Locations:**

**Artisan (225+):**
- Brikk Keencraft (Booty Bay, Stranglethorn Vale) - level 35 required

**Specializations:** Armorsmith, Weaponsmith

---

### Engineering

**Recommended Combination:** Mining

**Ore Farming References:**
- Copper Ore (1-75)
- Tin Ore (75-135)
- Iron Ore (135-200)
- Mithril Ore (200-250)
- Thorium Ore (250-300)

See Mining section for specific zone locations.

**Special Trainer Locations:**

**Artisan (225+):**
- Buzzek Bracketswing (Gadgetzan, Tanaris) - near inn mailbox, level 35+ required

**Specializations:** Gnome Engineering, Goblin Engineering

---

### Leatherworking

**Recommended Combination:** Skinning

**Farming Zones:** See Skinning section for leather farming locations

**Special Trainer Locations:**

**Expert (150+):**
- **Horde**: Una (Thunder Bluff)
- **Alliance**: Telonis (Darnassus)

**Artisan (225+):**
- **Horde**: Hahrana Ironhide (Camp Mojache, Feralas)
- **Alliance**: Drakk Stonehand (Aerie Peak, Hinterlands)

### Tailoring

**Recommended Combination:** Any gathering profession (cloth drops from humanoid mobs)

**Required Materials:**
- Linen Cloth
- Wool Cloth
- Silk Cloth
- Mageweave Cloth
- Runecloth

**Special Trainer Locations:**

**Artisan (225+):**
- **Horde**: Daryl Stack (Tarren Mill, Hillsbrad Foothills)
- **Alliance**: Timothy Worthington (Theramore Isle, Dustwallow Marsh)

**Notable Vendor:**
- Qia (Winterspring, Everlook - inside Bank) - sells limited-supply Runecloth Bag and Runecloth Gloves recipes

---

### Enchanting

**Special Farming Zone:**
- **Purple Lotus** (250-265): Needed for 40x Lesser Mana Oil enchants

**Special Trainer Locations:**

**Expert (150+, Level 20+):**
- **Horde**: Hgarth (Sun Rock Retreat, Stonetalon Mountains)
- **Alliance**: Kitta Firewind (Tower of Azora, Elwynn Forest)

**Artisan (225+, Level 35+):**
- Annora (Uldaman, Badlands instance) - ONLY trainer for this rank, both factions

## Secondary Professions

### Cooking

**Special Farming Zone:**
- **Silithus**: Farm 15x Sandworm Meat from Dredge Strikers and Dredge Crushers (alternative recipe for 275-300)

**Special Trainer Locations:**
- No special locations required - all ranks trainable in major cities

**Recipe Vendors:**
- Tanaris - multiple cooking recipes available

---

### Fishing

**Skill Level Progression & Zones:**

- **1-75**: Starter zones - Dun Morogh, Durotar, Elwynn Forest, Mulgore, Teldrassil, Tirisfal Glades

- **75-150**: Capital cities or zones like The Barrens, Darkshore, Ironforge, Loch Modan, Silverpine Forest, Westfall

- **150-225**: Dustwallow Marsh (recommended)
  - **Alternatives**: Alterac Mountains, Arathi Highlands, Desolace, Stranglethorn Vale, Swamp of Sorrows, Thousand Needles

- **225-300**: Felwood, Feralas, The Hinterlands, Tanaris, Un'Goro Crater, Western Plaguelands

**Special Trainer Locations:**

**Artisan (225+) - Quest Required:**
- **Nat Pagle** (Dustwallow Marsh) - Must complete his quest to advance past 225
  - Quest requires turning in rare fish from various zones

---

### First Aid

**Required Materials by Level:**
- **1-75**: Linen Cloth
- **75-150**: Wool Cloth
- **150-225**: Silk Cloth
- **225-300**: Mageweave Cloth, Runecloth

**Special Trainer Locations:**

**Artisan (225+):**
- **Horde**: Doctor Gregory Victor (Arathi Highlands)
- **Alliance**: Doctor Gustaf VanHowzen (Dustwallow Marsh)

---

### Lockpicking

**Class Restriction:** Rogue only

No trainers or farming zones - skill increases through practice on locked boxes, doors, and chests.

## Best Profession Combinations (for server with all professions)

**Gathering Trio:**
- Mining + Herbalism + Skinning = All raw materials

**Crafting:**
- Alchemy (uses Herbalism materials)
- Blacksmithing (uses Mining materials)
- Engineering (uses Mining materials)
- Enchanting (independent - disenchants items)
- Leatherworking (uses Skinning materials)
- Tailoring (independent - cloth from mobs)

**Secondary:**
- Cooking + Fishing + First Aid (all characters should get these)
